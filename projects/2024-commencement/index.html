COMMENCEMENT 2024

in → 2024
with → Ryan Brandon Hsiao, Peter Weicheng Xu
for → Rhode Island School of Design
deliverables → identity, poster, motion

Inspired by sketchbooks and process work, the visual identity for RISD’s 2024 Commencement embraces sparks of inspiration bursting forth on every page—like flowers, fireworks, and stars. Vibrant pinks and yellows complement RISD’s iconic blue, while playful hand lettering pairs with the bold, robust serif to balance spontaneity with structure.

Our motion language captures the energy of transition and celebration through a blend of stop motion and keyframe animation. Hand-drawn textures and organic movement highlight the process of making, resulting in a vibrant and dynamic visual system that enhances RISD’s established identity.
